Key Findings

  • • 14/23 (61%) are official PCAF signatories
  • • 21/23 (91%) mention or use PCAF methodology
  • • 12/23 (52%) report financed emissions (Part A) with quantified data
  • • 10/23 (43%) have partial Part A disclosure
  • • Only 2 institutions have critical compliance gaps

Glossary

PCAF — Partnership for Carbon Accounting Financials
DQS — Data Quality Score (1=best, 5=lowest)
Part A — Financed emissions (Scope 3 Cat. 15)
Part B — Facilitated emissions (banks)
Part C — Insurance-associated emissions
tCO2e — Tonnes of CO2 equivalent
MtCO2e — Million tonnes of CO2 equivalent
GHG — Greenhouse Gas
Scope 1 — Direct emissions from owned sources
Scope 2 — Indirect emissions from purchased energy
Scope 3 — All other indirect emissions in value chain
SBTi — Science Based Targets initiative
TCFD — Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures
ITV — Insurance Transaction Value method
