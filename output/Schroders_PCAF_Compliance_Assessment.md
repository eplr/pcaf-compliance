## Methodology notes

- **PCAF-aligned but not signatory**: L10130 explicitly says "own methodology, which is based on PCAF principles" — not a formal PCAF member. Score 3/5 (vs 4/5 for signatories).
- **S3 included**: Schroders reports S3 (141.9 MtCO2e) for listed equity+bonds+REITs+ETFs alongside S1&S2. This lifts listed equity to 6.0/6.0 pts.
- **Structural gaps**: No business loans, project finance, sovereign debt, mortgages, or motor vehicle loans — these are structurally absent from an asset manager's balance sheet, not a disclosure gap. Score 1/5 reflects the narrow scope of Schroders' business model for PCAF purposes.
- **No DQS, no coverage %**: Neither an aggregate PCAF DQS nor a portfolio coverage % is disclosed.
