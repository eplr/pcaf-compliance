## Methodology notes

- **PCAF signatory since 2023**: L929. Commerzbank meets all mandatory + voluntary PCAF requirements (L980–981). SBTi targets validated in 2023.
- **100% asset class coverage**: Commerzbank achieves the highest raw asset class coverage score in this batch (18/18), including full sovereign debt S3 reporting (L1008–1011) — a rare achievement shared only with Commerzbank.
- **CSRD first-time application**: 2024 defined as base year (L968). No comparable prior year financed emissions → Temporal Coverage limited to 1/3. Sector SBTi targets have 2021 base year but use intensity metrics (SDA), not absolute financed emissions.
- **Motor vehicle loans via mBank**: While Commerzbank AG excludes motor vehicle loans due to immateriality, subsidiary mBank reports this asset class (L1006–1008), ensuring the PCAF asset class is covered in the consolidated view.
- **Part C evaluated**: Insurance activities within NACE K.65 are small (€42M) but are included in the evaluation. No dedicated IAE methodology or time series exists → Part C scores minimal (3/13).
- **DQS discrepancy note**: Owned DQS S1&S2 = 4.0 falls in the 3.5–4.5 range (grid → Score 2). Axylia validated Score 3. Possible explanation: managed portfolio DQS (3.1) or alternative weighting methodology.
