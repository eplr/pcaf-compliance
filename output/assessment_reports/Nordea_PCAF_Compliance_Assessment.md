## Methodology notes

- **PCAF Board member since 2020**: Nordea is the most deeply PCAF-engaged bank in this assessment, with Board-level participation and membership in the PCAF Core Team and Nordic group (L25804). Only ING matches this level of PCAF involvement.
- **Project finance embedded**: Unlike most banks that separate project finance, Nordea embeds it in business loans (L28920). Full S1&S2 + S3 credit is given as the emissions are calculated per PCAF Part A.
- **Sovereign debt first year**: 2024 is the first year Nordea reports sovereign debt financed emissions (L28342). LULUCF included and excluded variants reported. S3 not yet available → 1.0/2.0 pts.
- **Documented deviations**: Nordea transparently documents deviations from PCAF (green production proxy, shipping Danish CO₂ model, E&P proxy). Each deviation improves data quality for Nordic-specific portfolios.
- **Facilitated emissions committed**: Nordea commits to future Part B reporting (L28348) but has not yet published data.
