### 2. Data Quality Score — 0/5

PCAF DQS scale described at L3704: *«Pour chaque calcul d’émissions financées, PCAF introduit un score de qualité de la donnée qui se situe entre 1 et 5. Un score de 1, le meilleur score, signifie que les données utilisées sont issues de données réelles du client financé et que le calcul des émissions de ce client a été vérifié par un tiers. Un score de 5, le score le plus bas, signifie que les données utilisées sont issues de données sectorielles et/ou régionales.»*

However, no weighted average DQS is explicitly disclosed for the portfolio. The report notes data quality heterogeneity (L4063: *«La qualité des données d’émission de GES est à ce stade encore très hétérogène»*). Per V3 rule (Score 0 = non-disclosed) → **Score 0/5**.

## Methodology notes

- **PCAF signatory confirmed**: L3697 explicitly states *«que le Groupe Crédit Agricole a rejoint [PCAF] en 2022»*. This is the clearest signatory confirmation found in any report in this batch.
- **Key corrections vs auto-generated file**: (1) Asset class coverage changed from 1/5 to **4/5** — listed equity/bonds, corporate loans, project finance, CRE, mortgages (residential), and motor vehicle loans all covered via PCAF methodology; (2) Attribution methodology changed from 2/5 to **4/5** — PCAF signatory since 2022 explicitly confirmed; (3) Portfolio coverage changed from **4/5 to 0/5** — the 89.2% at L22013 is the investment grade percentage of the credit portfolio, not PCAF portfolio coverage (critical error correction).
- **Retail loans in PCAF scope**: Mortgages and motor vehicle loans are excluded from the aggregate Cat 15 PCAF calculation (L4058: «prêts aux particuliers» excluded), but ARE covered by PCAF-based NZBA sector targets (L3691: «crédits aux entreprises et aux particuliers»). This gives credit for these asset classes under V3 scoring.
- **Documented PCAF deviation**: CA uses committed amounts («fonds engagés») instead of drawn amounts («fonds utilisés») per PCAF standard. This is explicitly noted at L3704 with justification.
- **Part B gap**: CA CIB is a top 5 global project finance bank. The absence of PCAF Part B facilitated emissions disclosure is a significant compliance gap.
